    How to Play

    Goal: Be the first to get four of your pieces in a row â€” horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

    Controls: Click a column to drop your piece (or use arrow keys to choose a column and Enter/Space to drop). The piece falls to the lowest available slot.

    Turns: Player 1 (red) and Player 2 (yellow) take turns. Red goes first.

    Winning: The first player to connect four wins. If the board fills up with no winner, the game is a draw.
